Toyota GR 86

The Toyota GR86 is a rear-wheel-drive sports coupe celebrated for its engaging driving dynamics and manual transmission option. Equipped with a 2.4-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder boxer engine, it delivers 228 horsepower and 184 lb.-ft. of torque, providing a responsive and spirited performance.

The 6-speed manual transmission features a short-throw shifter, enhancing driver engagement and control. This setup allows enthusiasts to fully experience the vehicle's performance capabilities, making it a popular choice among driving purists.

In terms of design, the GR86 boasts a low center of gravity and balanced weight distribution, contributing to its agile handling characteristics. The exterior features aerodynamic lines and a sporty stance, while the interior is driver-focused, offering supportive seating and intuitive controls.

Safety and technology are also integral to the GR86, with available features such as a digital gauge cluster and advanced driver-assistance systems, depending on the trim level. Overall, the Toyota GR86 with a manual transmission appeals to those seeking a pure and engaging driving experience.
